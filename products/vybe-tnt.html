FAQ

How do VYBE deep tanning oils work?

TAN ACCELERATOR: To speed up the process and tan your body faster than it would without them, the rapid tanVYBE formulas work by “attracting” the sun rays to your skin. This action allows your skin to get darker even after the first application, as the tanning effect multiplies over time.

TRIGGER MELANIN PRODUCTION: The rich complex of organic intensifier tan oils in all VYBE formulas stimulates melanin production. This leads to rapid formation of deep chocolate tan - for all skin types.

LONG-LASTING TAN: We know that hydrated skin tans several times faster than sun-dry skin. That’s why we enriched our tan accelerators with a mix of deeply moisturizing oils. They ensure that every nuance of choco skin you get is immediately “sealed in”!

What does VYBE TNT contain?

Almond Oil, Coconut Oil, Grape seed oil, Juniper essential oil, Avocado Oil, Sesame oil, Rosemary essential oil, DELIPIDOL.

What is the extra action of VYBE TNT and how does it work?

VYBE TNT is a double action formula - it speeds up suntanning and also immediately firms up the skin and tones the contours of the figure, enhancing the beautiful feminine curves.

Because it is enriched with a rich complex of vitamins E and A, VYBE TNT suntan oil strengthens the collagen and elastin fibers of the skin. This makes the skin visibly firmer and smoother - even with the first application! The effect of regular use multiplies over time, naturally eliminating any imperfections that you don’t want to bring to the beach.

How do I use VYBE TNT tanning oil?

First, shake well. Then pour the desired amount of your natural tanning oil into the palms of your hands, and spread and rub the oil evenly across the areas of your body you want tanned. If you are using it on more delicate skin areas like your face, be sure to do it gently and, again, evenly.

What makes the VYBE deep tanning formula so effective?

The VYBE formula was carefully designed especially for those who demand deep and long-lasting tanning - and also want it fast and natural.

To make that happen, we created a one-of-a-kind composition of the best tan-enhancing natural oils, combined with special extra hydrating ingredients. By naturally boosting melanin production, deeply moisturizing the skin and attracting the sunlight to it, the VYBE formula allows every skin type to achieve deep tan, fast and keep it even and saturated for months to come.

How long until I see the tanning results?

This will differ from person to person. It will depend on your natural skin tone and natural sun tanning ability. Some people have seen results when using the VYBE tan accelerator oils as quickly as in several hours; others who are fairer and have less melanin to begin with, may notice a slower increase.

Why should I apply aftersun lotion?

Hydrated skin tans several times faster than sun-dry skin. This action keeps your skin hydrated and soft at all times, which is vital to attaining more healthy tan and keeping it for longer. The effect multiplies over time so if you want to keep your choco tan well into the autumn months, it’s best if you keep using the hydrating balm even after you’ve stopped sunbathing.
